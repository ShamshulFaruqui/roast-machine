Hi Dadi(grandma in Urdu), I made an app that roasts you based on what you tell it, you can either describe yourself in text or upload your photo and it will make fun of you in a funny way. You can keep a chat going and fire back and keep the roasts going.
The AI doesnt just randomly insult you. Before it replies, it looks up a little knowledge base I built with tips on how to be actually funny, like 'a good roast has a kernel of truth' or 'be specific, not generic'. That way the roasts are clever instead of just rude. 
I also made it so if you try to trick it or say something harmful, it blocks you before it even reaches the AI.
I used the tools below to make the app: 
Groq - the AI that writes the roasts and looks at your photos
LangChain — connects everything together
Chroma — stores the comedy tips the AI reads before roasting
LangSmith — lets me see every AI call being made in real time, like a monitor
Streamlit — the website you actually see and click on
